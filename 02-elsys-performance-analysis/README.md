# Elsys - Sales & Commercial Intelligence Dashboard

## 🎯 Business Context & Problem
Analysis developed to monitor and evaluate operational sales performance, inventory turnover, and regional distribution for Elsys. The challenge was to transform decentralized commercial records into an executive, interactive view built entirely within spreadsheet environments for immediate business tracking.

## 🛠️ Technical Solution & Implementation
* **Data Cleansing & Normalization:** Handled missing data, formatted temporal metrics, and unified regional product codes using advanced spreadsheet functions.
* **KPI Architecture & Aggregation:** Built robust Pivot Tables and calculated core retail and operational metrics, including inventory turnover, conversion rates, and gross margin analysis.
* **Dashboard Engineering (Google Sheets):** Designed an interactive executive dashboard utilizing native visualization tools, dynamic trend lines for revenue forecasting, and conditional formatting to highlight seasonal revenue spikes and logistics bottlenecks.
